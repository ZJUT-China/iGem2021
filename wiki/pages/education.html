Education

The ancient Chinese sage Confucius once said, "Teach students according to their aptitude." As what he said,we divided the audience into four groups according to different ages and used different tones, different presentations, different scales to convey different knowledge to different groups of people.

Primary school

In China, until they enter high school students do not take specific "biology courses". Instead, they take "science courses" covering physics, chemistry, and biology. To let the young people have an independent concept of "biology" earlier, our ZJUT-China team contacted Qiushan Primary School in Deqing County through the Bioengineering College of Zhejiang University of Technology and gave a vivid biology lesson to the fourth-grade children. Primary school children are energetic and curious, so we chose to teach them in the form of PPT and performance. After establishing basic biological knowledge, we made safe paper models to let children understand the basic appearance of microorganisms.

At 2 pm on September 28th, our team members arrived at Qiushan Primary School. In the first part of our lesson, we taught the children about DNA and the basic concepts of genetic variation. Every time we asked, the children responded enthusiastically, "Why can't hen have baby tigers ?"Why do children look like their parents ?"Each question, stimulating children's curiosity at the same time, but also help them step by step to establish the correct biological concept. After the children used their new knowledge to solve problems for uncle Zhang, the farmer, we began to teach the children to make paper models. We chose Zika because its model is simple and easy to make, and doesn't have sharp horns. In the process of making models, children understand what microbes are and what they look like.

Middle school

Compared with primary school students, middle school students already have basic understanding of biology, so we can explain that our work to them in a more professional way. On August 29th, we participated in the summer camp for senior high school students sponsored by the School of Bioengineering, Zhejiang University of Technology. We explained the mechanics of our program to the high school students in an appropriate language. After our explanation, those students benefited a lot and had a deep understanding of our program and the differences between high school and university.

University

The education for college students is mainly aimed at freshmen. A lecture or activity is definitely beneficial to them. In May and September, we carried out an activity for them, namely the seminar of learning forum and the laboratory Open Day.

Study and Forum

On May 9th, our team was invited by the forum of Zhejiang University of Technology to give a lecture to all freshmen in Pingfeng Campus. The contents of the lecture include the introduction of iGEM Competition and the mechanism of ZJUT-China's current and previous projects. From the perspective of senior students, we analyzed the advantages of participating in iGEM to the freshmen and stimulated their enthusiasm for participating in the competition.

Laboratory Open Day

Experiments are an essential part of college life, so an offline laboratory experience for freshmen can not only lay a foundation for their future experimental operations but also gain freshmen's attention to our project. On September 11th, we held a laboratory Open Day for freshmen, aiming to cultivate freshmen's interest in professional knowledge through experiments and explanation of instruments.We have prepared four items for this experimental Open Day, which are visiting laboratory instruments, sampling and glue running, medium configuration, and the use of a pipette. There are more than 140 people joined laboratory open day in total.
